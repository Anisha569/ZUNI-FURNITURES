Commercial

Expert Commercial Fit Out Company

Designing a commercial interior which effectively balances considerations of function and productivity with those of brand image and aesthetics requires vision, experience, and expertise. These are all qualities that the House of Zunn team possess in spades and they have earned our company a reputation as the premier commercial fit out company. Creating an office or retail interior involves an entirely different design approach and a departure from many of the core concepts of residential design. An effective commercial interior is one which performs the dual role of engaging clients and facilitating the work of employees. To draw in a client, a commercial fit out must visually communicate the company’s brand identity, and invite the client in rather than make them feel like an alienated outsider. On the other hand, an office or retail interior should also be conducive to productivity, be easy to clean and maintain, and facilitate the business operations. Since 2003, House of Zunn has specialised in creating bespoke commercial interiors which meet these criteria and perfectly align with the needs, vision and expectations of our clients.

Interior Design and Home Furniture Solutions

Have the experienced team at House of Zunn create a bespoke living space which speaks to your taste and lifestyle. Since 2003, House of Zunn has been dedicated to providing interior design services which are tailored to the needs and aspirations of our clients. We believe one of the fundamental tenets of luxury design is personalisation and strive to create one of a kind living spaces that perfectly align with the tastes and lifestyle needs of our clients.

Outstanding interior design solutions tailored to suit your needs

When you choose to undertake a project with us you can expect a personalised service which foregrounds your needs and tastes. At House of Zunn, there is no one size fits all solution approach it comes to villa interior design and before any concepts are put forward, we take the time to learn about the needs, vision and expectations of our client. From there, we proceed with the conceptualisation, design, and renovation processes, ensuring we maintain a strong line of communication with our client to ensure the project conforms with their original vision. The end result is an exceptional interior which the client feels invested in and is able to enjoy for many years to come. Our bespoke design capabilities are further bolstered by the in- home furniture solutions we are able to offer. House of Zunn prides itself in manufacturing luxury furniture which is made to order and purpose built to suit the aesthetic and functional requirements of the home or villa we are working on.
